The Problem

"Abandoned animals often struggle to find food, water, and shelter, leading to malnutrition, dehydration, and susceptibility to diseases. Without proper veterinary care, they may suffer from untreated injuries or infections, Stray animals are vulnerable to accidents, such as being hit by cars, or attacks from other animals. They may also be exposed to environmental dangers like extreme weather conditions

Our story

The RSPCA’s history in Australia dates back to 1871, when a public meeting to discuss the ill treatment of horses in Victoria led to the formation of Australia’s first Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals. Following the establishment of a Society in Victoria, other states soon followed.
